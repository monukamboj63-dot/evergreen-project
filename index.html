Entry Zones

Explore different zones of the park, each offering unique wildlife viewing opportunities and distinct experiences. Choose your gateway to adventure!

Dhikala Zone

The most famous zone, known for its diverse wildlife and beautiful landscapes. Night stay is allowed in forest lodges.

Bijrani Zone

Popular for day safaris, Bijrani is rich in flora and fauna and offers a high chance of tiger sightings.

Jhirna & Dhela Zones

Open year-round, these zones are great for spotting sloth bears, leopards, and elephants.

Jeep Safari Info

Jeep safaris are the most popular way to explore Jim Corbett. Each jeep can accommodate up to 6 people and is accompanied by a trained guide. Safaris are available in the morning and evening. Advance booking is recommended, especially during peak season.

Canter Safari Info

Canter safaris are ideal for larger groups and families. The canter is an open bus that can seat up to 16 people. It is a safe and comfortable way to experience the park, especially for those who want to travel together.

Bird Watching Info

Jim Corbett is a birdwatcher's paradise with over 600 species of birds, including kingfishers, hornbills, eagles, and migratory birds. Guided birding tours are available for enthusiasts and beginners alike.

Elephant Ride Info

Elephant rides offer a unique vantage point to observe wildlife and the forest. Rides are conducted by trained mahouts and are subject to availability and park regulations.

Nainital Tour Info

Nainital, known as the City of Lakes, is a beautiful hill station just a short drive from Corbett. Enjoy boating on Naini Lake, panoramic views from Snow View Point, shopping on Mall Road, and visiting attractions like Naina Devi Temple, Tiffin Top, and Eco Cave Gardens. Perfect for families, couples, and nature lovers.
