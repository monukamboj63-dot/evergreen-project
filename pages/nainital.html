Nainital - The City of Lakes

Nainital is a beautiful hill station in Uttarakhand, famous for its emerald lakes, lush hills, and colonial charm. It is a perfect getaway for families, couples, and nature lovers, offering boating, shopping, and breathtaking views.

Top Places to Visit in Nainital

Naini Lake

The heart of Nainital, perfect for boating and surrounded by scenic hills and vibrant markets.

Naina Devi Temple

A sacred Hindu temple on the northern shore of Naini Lake, dedicated to Goddess Naina Devi.

Snow View Point

Offers panoramic views of the snow-capped Himalayas. Accessible by cable car or road.

Mall Road

A bustling street along the lake, lined with shops, cafes, and restaurants. Great for evening strolls.

Tiffin Top

A popular picnic spot offering panoramic views of Nainital and the surrounding hills.

Eco Cave Gardens

A network of interconnected caves and hanging gardens, fun for kids and families.

Kilbury and Pangot

Located about 12 kilometers from Nainital, Kilbury and Pangot are birdwatcher’s paradises. These spots are surrounded by dense oak, pine, and deodar forests.
